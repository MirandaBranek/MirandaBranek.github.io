About Us

For Goodness Bakes is a new, locally owned bakery that is ready to cater to all of your baking needs. We are ready to assist you in anyway possible, whether that be your everyday bakery items or even items for special events. Come see us in store to see what we have to offer!

Our Reason Why

We decided to open this bakery to allow our community to have the opportunity to come in and sit and relax and enjoy a freshly baked good. While also allowing people to have the flexibility to have freshly baked goods without having to drive out of town to obtain them.

We are Hiring!

Since we have just opened, we are still looking for a few employees to help us create some delicious baked goods! Even if you have not baked a day in your life, we are willing to train the right person. Contact us or stop in store to apply now.
